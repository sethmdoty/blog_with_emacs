General setup

Most of my personal computing is done on either a Macbook running the latest MacOS or a Lenovo Yoga c-940.

  • My Nix install runs https://i3wm.org/ as a esktop environment
  • My Mac dotfiles are managed with Nix.
  • I’m a big fan of NitroKeys for 2FA
  • Firefox as Web browser, with some extensions
    • Multi-Account Containers to isolate websites
    • uBlock Origin (Ad blocker)

  • Org-mode (“Your life in text mode”) on top of Emacs to organize notes, journals and information. It also powers this website.
